## Outreach Rules

- Use one exact-match anchor per placement at most; mix in branded anchors such as `MealEase`, `MealEase blog`, and `MealEase Snap & Cook`.
- Prioritize pages that can naturally cite MealEase as a tool, example, or practical workflow.
- Avoid paid link farms, generic directories, spun guest posts, and irrelevant coupon pages.
- Prefer contextual links inside useful content over sidebar, footer, or author bio links.
- Track source URL, target URL, anchor text, date requested, date live, and relationship owner.
