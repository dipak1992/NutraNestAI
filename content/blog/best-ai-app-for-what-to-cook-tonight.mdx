The best AI app for "what to cook tonight" should not make you write a perfect prompt.

It should already understand the dinner problem.

## Tonight has constraints

The app should ask or know:

- how much time you have
- what ingredients are available
- who is eating
- what leftovers need using
- whether the meal should be cheap
- what the household has eaten recently

That is the context behind the question.

## Recipe search is too broad

Searching recipes gives options. Tonight needs a decision.

MealEase Tonight Suggestions are built for that moment, and the broader [AI meal prep planner](/ai-meal-prep-planner) workflow connects tonight to the rest of the week.

## The best answer is actionable

A good tonight suggestion includes:

- the meal
- why it fits
- what you already have
- what is missing
- how long it takes
- what leftovers it creates

## The bottom line

Tonight does not need more inspiration.

It needs a dinner that fits your real kitchen.
