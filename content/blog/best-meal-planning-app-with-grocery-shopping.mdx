The best meal planning app is not the one with the most recipes.

It is the one that shortens the path between "what should we eat?" and "we have what we need."

## Recipe count is not the whole value

Most households do not fail because they lack recipes. They fail because planning, shopping, preferences, budget, and leftovers are disconnected.

A useful app connects those pieces.

## Grocery support to look for

Prioritize:

- automatic grocery lists from weekly plans
- editable grocery items
- pantry-aware planning
- copy and PDF export
- supported store handoff where available
- clear fallback for local shopping

This is where time savings become visible.

Choose a meal planning app that helps finish the grocery step. That is where dinner plans become real.
