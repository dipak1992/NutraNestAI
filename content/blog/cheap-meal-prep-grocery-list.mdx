A cheap meal prep grocery list should be boring in the best way.

It should rely on flexible staples that can become several dinners.

## Core staples

Start with:

- rice
- pasta
- tortillas
- eggs
- beans
- lentils
- frozen vegetables
- canned tomatoes
- chicken thighs
- yogurt
- apples or bananas
- salad greens

These ingredients create many meals without requiring specialty purchases.

## Build three dinners first

Use the list to make:

- rice bowls
- pasta with protein
- tacos or quesadillas

Then add one soup, one leftovers night, and one pantry backup.

## Avoid single-use ingredients

Expensive grocery weeks often come from ingredients used once.

If a sauce, cheese, herb, or spice appears in only one dinner, ask whether there is a cheaper swap.

## The bottom line

Cheap meal prep starts with flexible ingredients and a plan for using them twice.
