Cooked rice is one of the easiest leftovers to turn into meal prep.

It is cheap, filling, and flexible.

## Use rice as a base

Cooked rice can become:

- fried rice
- burrito bowls
- soup filler
- stuffed peppers
- crispy rice cakes
- rice breakfast bowls
- chicken and rice skillets

The key is adding protein, vegetables, and sauce.

## Store it safely

Cool rice quickly, refrigerate it promptly, and use it within a safe window. If it smells off or has been stored too long, skip it.

## Make it family-friendly

Keep sauces and toppings separate for picky eaters.

Rice bowls are especially useful because everyone can build a plate from the same base.

## The bottom line

Cooked rice is not a leftover problem.

It is a low-cost dinner starter.
