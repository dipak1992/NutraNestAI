Leftover rotisserie chicken is one of the best weeknight shortcuts.

It is already cooked, flexible, and easy to stretch into family dinners.

## Easy dinner formats

Try:

- chicken tacos
- chicken noodle soup
- BBQ chicken flatbreads
- chicken fried rice
- chicken Caesar wraps
- chicken pasta
- chicken quesadillas

These meals change the format enough that leftovers do not feel repetitive.

## Stretch small amounts

If you only have a little chicken, add:

- rice
- beans
- pasta
- eggs
- frozen vegetables
- tortillas
- broth

This turns a small container into dinner for more people.
