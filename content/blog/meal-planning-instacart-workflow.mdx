The best grocery workflow starts before the cart.

If you build the cart first, you still have to decide meals later. If you build the meal plan first, the grocery list has a purpose.

## The workflow

Use this order:

1. Pick dinners for the week.
2. Generate the grocery list.
3. Remove pantry items you already have.
4. Edit quantities and preferences.
5. Use supported store handoff where available.

Where supported, MealEase can help hand off grocery lists to providers such as Instacart.

## Why editing matters

Ingredient lists are not the same as grocery lists.

A real grocery list needs quantities, grouping, and common sense. You might want one larger container instead of three small ones. You might already have spices. You might want frozen vegetables instead of fresh.

## Keep expectations honest

MealEase helps organize and hand off grocery lists. Store checkout, substitutions, delivery windows, pickup, and payments are handled by the provider.

That keeps the experience useful without pretending the app controls the store.

## The result

Meal planning plus grocery handoff can turn Sunday planning from a two-hour chore into a much shorter weekly reset.
