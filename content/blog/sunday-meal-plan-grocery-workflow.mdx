A Sunday meal plan should make the week easier, not trap you in a plan that breaks by Tuesday.

The trick is to plan enough, then leave room.

## The 20-minute workflow

Use this sequence:

1. Check the calendar.
2. Pick the busiest night.
3. Add one leftovers night.
4. Choose three reliable dinners.
5. Add one pantry backup.
6. Generate the grocery list.
7. Remove what you already own.

That is enough for most family weeks.

## Do not plan every bite

Overplanning creates waste.

Leave space for leftovers, invitations, late practices, and low-energy nights.
