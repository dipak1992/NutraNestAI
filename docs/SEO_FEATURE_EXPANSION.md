## 1. STRATEGIC FOUNDATION

### Three Non-Negotiable Rules

1. **Every article links to a feature landing page** — not the homepage. Smart Menu Scan articles → `/dashboard/scan`. Food Check articles → `/dashboard/food-check`. Weekend Mode articles → `/dashboard` (Weekend Mode activates automatically).
2. **Every article has one primary CTA** tied to the specific feature it covers. No generic "try MealEase" CTAs on feature-specific content.
3. **Every article has a unique angle** — no "10 healthy foods" generics. Each article must answer a specific question a real person typed into Google.

### Conversion CTA Templates

**Smart Menu Scan:**
> "Want smarter restaurant picks instantly? Scan the menu with Smart Menu Scan — it reads any restaurant, fast food, or hotel menu and recommends your best option based on your goals."

**Food Check:**
> "Scan your meal and get instant calorie insights with Food Check — snap a photo of any food and get a calorie estimate plus healthy fit score in seconds."

**Weekend Mode:**
> "Weekend Mode unlocks every Friday at 11 AM — dinner + movie ideas tailored to your household, automatically. No setup needed."

### Search Intent Categories Used

- **Informational (I):** User wants to learn something
- **Commercial Investigation (CI):** User is comparing options before deciding
- **Transactional (T):** User is ready to act / download / sign up
- **Navigational (N):** User is looking for a specific brand/product
