### Post 15 — Vulnerability / Lesson Learned
**Type:** Text only
```
Biggest mistake I made building MealEase:

I gave users too many choices.

Version 1 showed 10 dinner options.
Nobody could decide. (Ironic, right?)

Version 2 shows ONE recommendation.
With a "swap" button if you don't like it.

Conversions went up 340%.

The lesson: Your users don't want options.
They want answers.

If your product makes people think MORE, you've failed.
If it makes them think LESS, you've won.
```
